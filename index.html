A Bit About Me

I graduated from the University of Denver in 2020 with a BA in International Studies and Philosophy, and minors in Arabic and Religous Studies. And then...I did an MA specializing in Quantitative Analysis/Data Science? Correct! After realizing my passion for data while studying abroad in Zurich, Switzerland, working for Dr.Kai Gehring, I became set on pursuing a career related to data science, data visualization, and machine learning.

Currently, I work for HP - previously Poly, until acquired in November 2022. One of my favorite projects I worked on while at Poly was conducting natural language processing on customer service tickets utilizing the BERT algorithm. While working on this project, I worked in a multi-developer environment within Gitlab, used AWS S3, EC2, and Lambda functions for data management, as well as wrote CI/CD for the project, conducted unit testing, and refactored code. Perhaps my favorite aspect of this project was working with a much more senior data scientist; I learned more working with him than I ever could have even expected. Other aspects of this position include jointly supporting Pricing Strategy and Data Science teams, leveraging statistical and machine learning techniques to address business problems, performing data engineering tasks to automate data flows, and build dashboards to support decision-making, and partnering with stakeholders to identify requirements and deliverables.

During the summer of 2020, I interned for ArcherDx as a regulatory affairs intern. While working there, I was able to a work in R's Shiny package, and create a Quality Control Shiny application in order to analyze complaint data for their next-generation sequencing biotechnology.

I then worked as the Lead BI Developer with the Science Applications International Corp (SAIC) J5 A&E dashboarding team for the Military Health System's (MHS) Defense Health Agency. Day to day I worked with Python, SQL Server, and Tableau in order to push data-driven decisions regarding MHS policy and patient safety.

But hey! You can still see my personal projects above!

In my free time, I tend not to stop...ever. I ski mountaineer - well, split-board mountaineer actually - check out my Instagram! I also love to road bike, rock climb, and played Club Volleyball for the University of Denver during my undergraduate studies. Often times you'll find my friends and I having Settlers of Catan nights, or working at various coffee shops around Denver.
