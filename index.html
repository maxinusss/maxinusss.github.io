A Bit About Me

I graduated from the University of Denver in 2020 with a BA in International Studies and Philosophy, and minors in Arabic and Religous Studies. And now...I'm doing an MA specializing in Quantitative Analysis/Data Science? Correct! After realizing my passion for data while studying abroad in Zurich, Switzerland, working for Dr.Kai Gehring, I became set on pursuing a career related to data science and data visualization. This past summer, I interned for ArcherDx as a regulatory affairs intern. While working there, I was able to a work in R's Shiny package, and create a Quality Control Shiny application in order to analyze complaint data for their next-generation sequencing biotechnology.
